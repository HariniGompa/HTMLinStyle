Nine noble families wage war against each other in order to gain control over the mythical land of Westeros. Meanwhile, a force is rising after millenniums and threatens the existence of living men.

GOT Season 1

The power struggles in Westeros as Eddard Stark is appointed as the King’s Hand, uncovering dangerous secrets in the capital. Meanwhile, Daenerys Targaryen begins her journey to reclaim the Iron Throne, and ancient dangers stir beyond the Wall.

GOT Season 3

The Stark family suffer devastating losses, including the infamous Red Wedding, while Daenerys Targaryen gains power in Slaver's Bay. The season intensifies the battle for the Iron Throne as alliances shift and new enemies emerge.

GOT Season 5

Expanding conflict across Westeros and Essos, with Daenerys struggling to maintain control in Meereen and Cersei facing political and personal downfall in King's Landing. Jon Snow’s leadership at the Wall is tested as the looming threat of the White Walkers grows ever closer.

House of Dragons

After the death of their father, two siblings fight for the throne, thereby causing a civil war known as the Dance of the Dragons.

The Witcher

The witcher Geralt, a mutated monster hunter, struggles to find his place in a world in which people often prove more wicked than beasts.

His Dark Materials

Lyra, a young orphan from an alternate world, stays with the scholars in a college in Oxford. During a quest to find her missing friend, she discovers a secret that involves several kidnappings.
